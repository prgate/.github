# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

PRGate is committed to a respectful, constructive, and inclusive community.

Participants in this project are expected to:

- be respectful in discussion, review, and collaboration
- assume good intent while still giving direct technical feedback
- focus criticism on ideas, designs, and code rather than people
- avoid harassment, discrimination, intimidation, and personal attacks

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- abusive or insulting language
- personal or political attacks
- sustained disruption of discussions or reviews
- sharing private information without permission
- any conduct that would make participation unsafe or hostile

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that do not follow this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this policy, use a private reporting channel listed in the affected repository when one exists. If no private channel is listed yet, contact the maintainers at `k@kazakov.xyz`.
